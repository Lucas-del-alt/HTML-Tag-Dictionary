h1: header tag, titles headings

ex:<h1>Lucas</h1>

h2: smaller header tag (h1-h6), good for subheadings

a: "anchor" makes link clickable
attributes: href
ex: <a href="link">

img: attaches an image
attributes: src
ex: <img src="frog.jpg"> 

li: makes a list, can be only be used alongside ol or ul

<li> playing video games</li>

ol: makes an ordered list, must be used alongside li

<ol><li>homework</li><ol>

p: makes a paragraph/ block of text

<p> aaaaaa </p>

ul: makes a bulleted list, must be used alongside li

<ul><li>videogames</li><ul>
